The Best Movies According to Janno

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spirited Away

One of the first and best anime movie I watched. It involves
a story about a little girl getting lost in a magical world.

Blade Runner 2049

When it comes to the Cyberpunk Genre, this movie is my go to
when I have that itch to watch one.

The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe

When it comes to the fantasy setting, this is the first movie that comes
to mind as it one of the first movies that captivated my young self
in terms of getting lost in a medievial fantasy world.
